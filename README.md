SALES DATASET PROJECT
1️⃣ Introduction

Sales data analysis helps businesses understand how products perform in the market. By analyzing sales data, companies can identify trends, measure performance, and make better decisions.

In this project, we analyze a simple sales dataset that contains:

Quantity Sold

Unit Price

Total Sales

The main objective is to calculate total revenue and understand overall sales performance.

2️⃣ Dataset Overview

The dataset contains the following variables:

Column Name	Description
Quantity Sold	Number of units sold
Unit Price	Price per unit (₹)
Total Sales	Quantity × Unit Price
Example Dataset:
Product	Quantity Sold	Unit Price (₹)	Total Sales (₹)
P1	10	50	500
P2	5	100	500
P3	8	75	600
P4	12	60	720
P5	7	90	630

Overall Sales = ₹2950

3️⃣ Methodology

The following steps were followed:

Data Collection – Created sample sales data.

Data Processing – Converted data into arrays using NumPy.

Calculation – Computed total sales using formula:

Total Sales = Quantity Sold × Unit Price

Aggregation – Calculated overall revenue using sum function.

Analysis – Measured mean, maximum, and minimum values.

4️⃣ Statistical Analysis

Using basic statistical methods:

Mean (Average) Sales
= Total Sales / Number of Products
= 2950 / 5 = ₹590

Maximum Sales = ₹720

Minimum Sales = ₹500

Total Revenue = ₹2950

These statistics help identify:

Best performing product (P4)

Least performing product (P1 & P2)

Overall revenue trend

5️⃣ Conclusion

The sales dataset analysis shows that:

Product P4 generated the highest revenue.

The overall revenue is ₹2950.

The average revenue per product is ₹590.

Statistical analysis helps in identifying top-performing and low-performing products.

Sales data analysis is important for business growth, forecasting, and strategic planning
